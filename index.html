What I know

Responsive web design

Ensuring that the content of the web is accessible to everyone in a comfortable way regardless of the size of the device they use or if they have a disability is essential and is not an option. I use the knowledge gained from a self-taught way, as well as my own user experience to achieve this.

Web Development

The best way to develop software is to do it in a declarative way, and to start thinking directly about the end user of the content before anything else, and that is the reason why I have specialized in Front-end working with React, Vue, Sass Bootstrap, Tailwind and Typescript, and in the Back-end working with Laravel and PHP. This languages and frameworks are the perfect tools to achieve this goal.

Programming courses and blogs

What good is knowledge if it's not shared? Since I have learned almost everything I know from people who have shared their knowledge on the internet, I like to try to give back a little of everything they have given me and help others learn useful things about programming, as well as share the things that I learn over time, you should never stop learning!
