Know me

Hello, my real name is Kevin Enrique Loera Salazar, I am currently 25 years old and I am from Mexico.

Since I can remember, I have always had a great affinity for computers, even my mother often tells me that I wanted to create robots and she talked about it all the time.

My first contact with software development was more or less in 2014, I tried to do a video game programming course with Unity using monodevelop and C#, but honestly it was too difficult, today I know that it is one of the most complex areas in the software development. development but the good side is that that only gave me determination to start something simpler.

Shortly after I started learning C++ with the book C++ Primer Fifth Edition. What I like most about software development is the feeling of creating things from nothing, it is almost magical, you write a spell and something appears that a few hours ago did not exist and with which you can interact, and above all, it solves a problem.

Over time I also learned other technologies such as Python, GoLang, C#, HTML, CSS, JS, React, Typescript, Vue, PHP and Laravel. Although I have also tried other things, such as video game development with the GML or Python language using Godot, or development for Arduino with C.

I also plan not only to finish the Python course that I currently have on my Youtube channel, but to do more courses, as well as podcast on different topics.
